This is a basic demonstration of the Sunburst Diagram.
Overview
The Sunburst Diagram is sometimes refered to as a Ring Chart, Multi-level Pie Chart, Belt Chart or Radial Treemap. This visualization depicts a hierarchy as a series of concentric rings divided into sections typically proportional to some quantitative association within the hierarchy. Each ring represents a level of the hierarchy with the root node at the center and leaf nodes at the outer rings.
